Minoli connects with the Capital at ‘The Surface Within’.

The Surface Within is Minoli’s West End showcase for the very best of the company’s new and established collections, and features extensive displays, samples and product information.

In designing the space, we thought carefully about the needs of Minoli’s varied customer base, which includes house builders, developers, designers, corporate clients, resellers (retailers) and the automotive sector.

Inside they will find a thoughtfully designed presentation suite, the main gallery with large format displays and room settings, plus formal and more relaxed meeting spaces with Wi-Fi and a great cup of coffee.

The lower-ground floor has more of a studio feel with further display rooms and a substantial line-up of samples arranged around a purpose-built work station providing the perfect place to create schemes and fine tune selections. The Retreat is a more informal meeting space, situated to enable clients to make selections in comfort and privacy, ideal for the designer working with a private client.    

“The Surface Within is a logical extension to our existing Oxford-based headquarters and distribution centre. We’ve have serviced the important and vibrant, ever-expanding market that the capital provides and this new showroom and resource centre means we can offer the same excellent levels of customer service in the very heart of London as well.”
Jonathon Minoli, Managing Director
