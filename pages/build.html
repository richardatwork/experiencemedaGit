  • What we build.

    When you’re literally building to deliver your experience, you want to ensure the places you create measure up; qualitatively for customers and employees, quantitively for investors and yields; and for the best possible returns on the investment in your products, services and brands.

    Meda understand that while the architectural design intent of building projects is often inspiring in concept, it’s hugely demanding to achieve - and consistently, across networks - within budget constraints.

    That’s why we specialise in the effective delivery that your ambition and expectations demand; retaining impact and intent, from a deep understanding of both.

    We’re partner of choice for some of the world’s biggest, best-known and demanding brands, who entrust us with the design and management of their most critical projects. In many cases we literally ‘set the standards’.

    With experience across a wide range of sectors, and unique expertise in the retail automotive arena, we have a proven international track record of delivering buildings that are in budget, on time and to specification. Our in-house team comprises architects, project managers and quantity surveyors providing a truly unified solution for your project.

    Whether assessing site suitability, project feasibility, implementing architectural design language or providing consistency for an international network of sites, we take time to understand your particular requirements, ensuring that the end result delivers on your brief.

    With an unrivalled understanding of retail and commercial behaviours and processes, we’re also diligent at ensuring any creative aspirations are balanced with your commercial requirements and budget constraints.

    The delivery of global concepts, consistently into mainland European and UK markets, meeting the varying needs of brands and a wide variety of operator types is what we do best.

    Above all, we’re committed to improving your reputation and results.
